Do's and don'ts

Do

  • Use Cascadia Green for navigation and structural elements
  • Use the warm accent for interactive elements and highlights
  • Keep border-radius at 6-8px
  • Use sentence case for all headings
  • Keep line-heights generous on body text (1.50+)
  • Design for mobile-first interaction
  • Use the Fog / Overcast / Snow scale for depth without shadows

Don't

  • Use Cascadia Green or Blue for large interactive surfaces
  • Uppercase anything except table headers and badges
  • Go below 6px or above 8px radius (except pills)
  • Use heavy font weights (700+) — 500 is the max
  • Introduce colors outside the flag-derived palette
  • Over-shadow — reach for borders or background shifts first
